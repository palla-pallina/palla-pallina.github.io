about tintaPH          


tintaPH is a vibrant, Philippines-centric social media platform designed exclusively for artists of all backgrounds and skill levels. Our supportive and inclusive community celebrates creativity in all its forms, welcoming everything from painting and photography to sculpture and digital art.

Whether you’re a seasoned professional or just starting your artistic journey, you’ll find a nurturing space to share your work, gain feedback, and connect with fellow creators who are equally passionate about visual art. Join us and be part of a dynamic network that inspires and uplifts artists across the country!
